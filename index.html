Lightning Terminal

The Cashup Terminal lets you scan your LNURL payment code, enter amounts in your local currency, and receive payments with real-time verification. Your payment history is tracked in a local database and can be exported as a CSV at any time. You can use this terminal to connect to any LNURL compatible Lightning wallet such as Conduit or Blink.

Download for Android

What is LNURL?

LNURL is a protocol that simplifies Lightning payments by defining a protocol for third parties to generate invoices from your wallet on demand. Cashup Terminal can use any LNURL that supports the LUD21 extension to generate invoices and verify whether they have been paid.
